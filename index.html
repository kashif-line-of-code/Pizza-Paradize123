Our Story

Since 1985, Pizza Paradise has been serving the community with authentic Italian pizzas made from traditional recipes passed down through generations.

We use only the finest ingredients, from our specially imported Italian tomatoes to our hand-stretched dough that's proofed for 48 hours.

Our wood-fired ovens reach temperatures of 900°F, baking our pizzas in just 90 seconds for that perfect crisp crust.
