Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 11am to 10pm Monday through Thursday, 11am to 11pm Friday and Saturday, and 12pm to 9pm on Sundays.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes! We offer gluten-free crust options for an additional $2. Please note that while we take precautions, our kitchen handles gluten-containing ingredients.

How can I track my delivery order?

After placing your order, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also call us directly at (302) 555-8633 for updates.

Do you cater events?

Absolutely! We offer catering services for events of all sizes. Contact our events team at events@kashiflaskani23@gmail.com for customized catering options.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal for online orders.
