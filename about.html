From Italy with Love

Since 1985, Pizza Paradise has been serving the community with authentic Italian pizzas made from traditional recipes passed down through generations. Our founder, Giovanni Rossi, brought his family's century-old pizza-making traditions from Naples to create a truly authentic experience.

We use only the finest ingredients, from our specially imported Italian tomatoes to our hand-stretched dough that's proofed for 48 hours. Our wood-fired ovens reach temperatures of 900°F, baking our pizzas in just 90 seconds for that perfect crisp crust.

"At Pizza Paradise, we believe that great pizza is more than just food—it's a celebration of family, tradition, and the joy of sharing a meal together."

- Giovanni Rossi, Founder

Today, under the leadership of Giovanni's daughter, Maria, we continue to honor these traditions while innovating with new flavors that reflect our diverse community. Every pizza tells a story, and we're proud to share ours with you.

Our Journey

2000

Giovanni Rossi opens the first Pizza Paradise with recipes from his grandmother in Naples.

2005

We introduce our wood-fired oven, becoming the first pizzeria in the city to use this traditional method.

2010

Pizza Paradise wins "Best Pizza in the City" award for the first time.

2015

Maria Rossi takes over the business, bringing fresh ideas while honoring tradition.

2020

We launch our community outreach program, donating pizzas to local shelters and hospitals.

2025

Expansion begins with plans for a second location while maintaining our commitment to quality.
